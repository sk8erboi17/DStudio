# SKILL: blog

This is about reading. The single most important decision is the **measure** (line length)
and the **type**. Everything else serves the eye moving down the page without fatigue.

## The article

- **Measure**: 60-75 characters per line (~"max-width: 68ch"). Wider is unreadable; narrower
  is choppy. The body column is centered; everything hangs off it.
- **Type**: body 18-21px, line-height 1.6-1.75. A serif (Georgia/Charter) or a humanist
  sans for body reads well at length. Headings can contrast (different family/weight).
- **Vertical rhythm**: consistent paragraph spacing, real `<h2>/<h3>` hierarchy, generous
  space before headings. Don't justify text (rivers); left-align.
- **Lead**: title (large), a one-line dek, byline + date + reading time, then the piece.
- **Rich content done right**: blockquotes with a real treatment (rule or indent, not just
  italics), figures with captions, inline code + code blocks (monospace, subtle fill),
  lists with breathing room, the occasional full-bleed image that breaks the measure.
- **Drop the chrome while reading**: minimal header, no sidebar fighting the text; a thin
  reading-progress bar is a nice touch.
- **Footer of the piece**: author bio card, share, prev/next or related posts.

## The index (if asked)

A list/grid of posts: each card = title, dek, date + reading time, tag, optional thumbnail.
The newest/featured post can be larger (a hero). Keep it scannable; the title is the link.

## Craft

- **Hierarchy through type, not boxes** — size, weight and space separate sections, not
  borders everywhere.
- **Images earn their place** with captions and good aspect ratios; never decorative stock.
- **Dark mode reads well** for long text — offer it or design a warm, low-glare light.
- A **table of contents** (sticky, for long pieces) helps; highlight the current section.

## Self-check before artifact (fix anything < 3/5)

- **Readability**: is the measure 60-75ch and the body ≥18px with 1.6+ line-height?
- **Rhythm**: clear heading hierarchy and consistent paragraph spacing?
- **Restraint**: is the chrome out of the way while reading?
- **Rich elements**: do quotes/code/figures have real, considered treatments?
- **Finish-ability**: would you actually read to the end?

Gate: contrast ≥4.5:1 (text on bg, generous), no justified body, images have captions, the
column reflows cleanly at 390, no lorem in headings/dek.
